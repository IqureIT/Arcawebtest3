The Story Of Therapia and Assisted Living

During a journey through remote villages, the founder witnessed how ancient remedies and community support transformed lives despite limited resources. Inspired by the resilience of people and the healing power of nature, Therapia was born to bridge traditional wisdom with modern science. This vision extended to creating assisted living devices, ensuring dignity, comfort, and care for all in need.
